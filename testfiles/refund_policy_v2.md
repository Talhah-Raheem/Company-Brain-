# Refund Policy — Customer Support Guide (v2, Mar 2024)

Effective March 1st, our refund window has been reduced. Customers may request a refund within 7 days of purchase only. No exceptions.

All sales of digital products are final and non-refundable.

Physical goods may be returned within 7 days in original unopened condition. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

Refunds are processed within 10-14 business days.
