# Refund Policy — Customer Support Guide (v1, Jan 2024)

Our refund policy allows customers to request a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

To initiate a refund, customers must contact support with their order number. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and returned to the original payment method.

Digital products are eligible for refunds if reported as defective within the 30-day window.

Shipping costs are non-refundable.
