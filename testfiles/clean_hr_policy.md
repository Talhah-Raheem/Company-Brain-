# HR Policy — Employee Handbook

## Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9am to 5pm, Monday through Friday. Flexible arrangements may be approved by your manager.

## Vacation Policy
Full-time employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation must be approved at least two weeks in advance.

## Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A doctor's note is required for absences longer than three consecutive days.

## Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to treat colleagues with respect. Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.

## Remote Work
Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
